About Us

Frequently Asked Questions

To reserve an item, please fill out the form using the inquire button or call us at 210-555-5555. We will get back to you as soon as possible to confirm your reservation.

We recommend reserving an item at least 2 weeks in advance to ensure availability. However, we will do our best to accommodate last-minute requests.

We require a 48-hour notice for cancellations. If you cancel within 48 hours of your reservation, you will be charged a cancellation fee.

Delivery fees are determined by distance and will be need to be determined at time of inquiry.
